HIPAA Compliance

Patient data protection isn't optional — it's our advantage

HIPAA compliance is hard. Most translation tools sidestep it by avoiding patient data entirely. We built ClearVoice to handle real patient calls with full compliance from day one.

Every vendor in the chain has a BAA

From the phone carrier to the speech engine to the server — every piece that touches patient audio is covered by a signed Business Associate Agreement.

No data stored, no risk

Audio is translated in real time and never recorded or stored. Patient data flows through the system and is gone — minimizing exposure and simplifying compliance.

Built for healthcare from day one

ClearVoice isn't a general translation tool adapted for healthcare. It was designed by a physician who lives this problem every day.
